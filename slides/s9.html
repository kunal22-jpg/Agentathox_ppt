Social Impact Dimensions

Closing Treatment Gap

75% of Indian patients receive no care. NUTRACIA offers free, always-on AI therapy, removing cost and stigma barriers for Tier-2/3 cities.

Democratizing Wellness

Professional nutrition & fitness guidance usually costs ₹3k-8k/month. We deliver expert-level plans at ₹0, powered by medical-grade AI.

Bridging Discovery

Turning Google Maps into a mental health navigation tool. Finding a verified nearby therapist takes 10 seconds, not days of searching.

Preventive Health

Proactive management of diet, sleep, and stress reduces the long-term risk of lifestyle diseases (diabetes, hypertension) in young adults.

Emotional Awareness

Smile Detection and Anne's Diary build daily self-reflection habits, normalizing mental health check-ins among populations where it's taboo.

Population Intelligence

Aggregated, anonymized mood and wellness data provides researchers with the first granular dataset on Indian youth mental health trends.
